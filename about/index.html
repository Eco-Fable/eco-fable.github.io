About the Author

I’m a twenty-something trying to find her place in this vast, scary, and sometimes wonderous universe. I have a Master in Environmental Management, and have worked with state agencies and a variety of environmental nonprofits. I’m interested in wildlife conservation, habitat management, and environmental communication. I have experience working in the field using camera traps, conducting water quality tests, and native/invasive vegetation assessments. I’ve also analyzed data using software/programs like ArcGIS, QGIS, Circuitscape, Maxent, and R.

I enjoy reading, art history, learning about wildlife, folklore/mythology, and writing. I am currently working as a state seasonal technician focusing on forest health.

I also work nights managing New England’s largest collection of vampire hunting memorabilia.

I’m actually a freelance crypto-consultant. I spent this past fall tracking Connecticut’s elusive Glawackus, and am now helping a neighbor negotiate rent for a family of squatting fairies in her attic.
